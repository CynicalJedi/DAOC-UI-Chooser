Merchant Hookpoint

 

The Merchant Hookpoint window is used to interact with a Merchant Hookpoint.

 

The window will appear when you right click on a hookpoint circle in a central keep or tower.  The window is re-sizeable so you can see all the items available on the merchant at once.  Your Personal Coin and Personal Bounty Point levels are shown in the window along with your Guild's Bounty Point levels.  The listbox in the middle of the window will show you the current items that may be purchased at the current hookpoint.  Different items will appear in the window depending on which hookpoint was selected on the keep or tower.  The Personal Coins, Personal BPoints, Contract, and Guild BPoints buttons are used to toggle the method of payment for the selected item.  The Buy button is used to purchase the selected item.

 

Note:  If the hookpoint circles are not visible, hold down the shift key and right click on the keep or tower.  When the Keep Status window appears click on the Hookpoint button at the bottom of the window to toggle the visibility of the hookpoint circles on or off.

 

- Conceal

There is nothing shown when the window is concealed.  The window is invisible with no graphics or controls to minimize the amount of memory used.  Nothing will appear on the screen when you attempt to activate the window.
