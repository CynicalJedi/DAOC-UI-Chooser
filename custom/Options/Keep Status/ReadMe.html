Keep Status

 

The Keep Status window is used to display information about a keep or tower.

 

The window will appear when you right click on a keep or tower section.  The hookpoints button is used to toggle the display of hookpoints on the keep or tower.  The Repair button is used to repair the section of the keep or tower currently selected.

 

- Conceal

There is nothing shown when the window is concealed.  The window is invisible with no graphics or controls to minimize the amount of memory used.  Nothing will appear on the screen when you attempt to activate the window.
