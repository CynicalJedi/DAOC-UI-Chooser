Master Level

 

The Master Level window is used to display Master Level information.

 

The window will appear when you click on the button labeled "Master Lvl" in the Main Menu or Command windows of the user interface.  In addition you can type the command "/Master" to activate the window.  Your current Master Level Title and Rank is shown at the top of the display.  The steps needed to complete the Master Levels are shown in the middle of the display.  The left and right buttons at the bottom of the display are used to toggle through the information for each step.

 

- Conceal

There is nothing shown when the window is concealed.  The window is invisible with no graphics or controls to minimize the amount of memory used.  Nothing will appear on the screen when you attempt to activate the window.
