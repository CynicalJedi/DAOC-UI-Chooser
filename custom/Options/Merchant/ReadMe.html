Merchant

 

The Merchant window is used to interact with a Merchant NPC.

 

The window will appear when you right click on a merchant NPC.  The window is re-sizeable so you can see all the items available on the merchant at once.  The Quantity up and down buttons are used to change the amount of items to purchase.  You can hold down the shift key while clicking on the Quantity buttons to increase or decrease the quantity by ten.  The Keep Quantity radio button is used to "remember" the quantity of items for purchase between transactions.  The Buy button is used to purchase the entered quantity of the selected item.  The Sell button is used to sell the selected item in your backpack.  The Appraise button is used to request the sellback price of the selected item in your backpack.

 

- Conceal

There is nothing shown when the window is concealed.  The window is invisible with no graphics or controls to minimize the amount of memory used.  Nothing will appear on the screen when you attempt to activate the window.
