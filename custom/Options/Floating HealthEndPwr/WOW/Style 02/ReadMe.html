Floating HealthEndPwr - WOW - Style 2

 

This option allows you to choose this style for the Floating Health, Endurance, and Power windows.

 

This is similar to the Player with Pet window used in WOW.  Currently, the game client only provides information about your pet's health.  It does not provide information about your pet's endurance, and power status like in WOW.  It also does not provide your pet's level as a separate item but it is embedded with the pet's name.

 

Small widgets appear at the bottom of the window to indicate group member health and combat status.  A caution indicator appears whenever one of your group member's health drops below 75%.  A combat indicator appears when you enter combat mode.  In addition a combat frame appears around the health globe and player's name when you are in combat mode.  Colored "dots" are used to indicate group member's health status.  Health status colors in order of highest to lowest are blue, green, yellow, red, and blank when dead or not available.  The target window is similar to the one in WOW. Currently, the game client only provides information about your target's health.  It does not provide information about your target's level, endurance, and power status like in WOW.
