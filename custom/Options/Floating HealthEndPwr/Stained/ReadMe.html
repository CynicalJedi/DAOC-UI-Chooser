Floating HealthEndPwr - Stained

 

These are the Stained style Floating Health, Endurance, and Power windows.

 

Style 1

With this style the Player's Health, Endurance, and Power status are shown in individual Floating Health, Endurance, and Power windows.

 

 

Style 2

With this style the Player's Endurance and Power status are combined on the Floating Endurance window.  The Floating Power window is then used to show the Player's Concentration status.

 

 

Style 3

With this style the Player's Health and Endurance are combined on the Floating Health window.  The Player's Power status is shown on the Floating Power window.  The Floating Endurance window is then used to show the Player's Concentration status.

 

 

Style 4

With this style the Player's Health, Endurance, and Power status are shown in individual Floating Health, Endurance, and Power windows.

 

 

Style 5

With this style the Player's Health, Endurance, Power, and Concentration status are shown in individual Floating Health, Endurance, and Power windows.
