Style 7

This option allows you to choose this style for the Floating Health window.  This style is for use by Necromancers whose life is linked directly to their pet's life.  The health bar is linked to the pet's life.  The smaller bar beneath the pet's life is linked to the player's health.

 

 

Style 8

This option allows you to choose this style for the Floating Health window.  This style is for use by Necromancers whose life is linked directly to their pet's life.  The health bar is linked to the pet's life.  The smaller bar beneath the pet's life is linked to the player's health.

 

The name of your pet is NOT shown above the health bar in this style window.

 

 

 

Note: The status bar skins can be changed using the choices in the Status Bar Skins option.

 

Note: There are some invisible buttons in the Floating Health windows.

1. Click on the name to FACE your current target.

2. Click on the health bar to STICK to your current target.
