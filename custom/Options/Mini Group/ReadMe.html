Mini Group

 

There are several styles for the Mini Group windows that you can choose.

 

The window will appear when you click on the button labeled "Mini Grp" in the Main Menu or Command windows of the user Interface.  Many of the window styles will appear invisible if you are not currently in a group when the window is opened.

 

The Mini Group window is used to track group member's health, endurance, and power levels.  Many of the various styles are resizable so you can reduce the amount of screen space taken by the window of your group is not full.

 

Many of the windows have a "highlight" frame that appears around a group member's information when you hover over it with your mouse.  When the frame appears you can left click on the group member to select them as your current target.  With the WOW style windows click on the health bubble to select the group member.

 

- Conceal

There is nothing shown when the window is concealed.  The window is invisible with no graphics or controls to minimize the amount of memory used.  Nothing will appear on the screen when you attempt to activate the window.
