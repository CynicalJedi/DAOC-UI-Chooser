Mini Craft

 

The Mini Craft window is used to repair, craft, or salvage an item in your backpack.

 

The Mini Craft window will appear when you select an item in your backpack and then press Shift R (default).  Check your keyboard configuration if the window does not appear.

 

- Conceal

There is nothing shown when the window is concealed.  The window is invisible with no graphics or controls to minimize the amount of memory used.  Nothing will appear on the screen when you attempt to activate the window.
